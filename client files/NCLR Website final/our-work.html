About us

 

We reach millions of Hispanics in 41 states

 

 

Our Philosophy

 

 

 

 

At the National Council of La Raza (NCLR) we work to improve opportunities for Hispanic Americans. Founded in 1968, we are the the largest national Hispanic civil rights and advocacy organization in the United States, and we reach millions of Latinos each year through our nationwide network of nearly 300 affiliated community-based organizations. Our headquarters are in Washington, DC, and we have state and regional offices in Chicago, Los Angeles, Miami, New York, Phoenix, and San Antonio.

We are focused on improving the lives of the Latino community and all Americans through our work on civil rights and immigration, education, employment and the economy, health, and housing issues.
