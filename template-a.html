Civil Rights

We promote and protect equality of opportunity in voting, justice issues, education, employment, housing, and health care for all Americans.

Economy/
Workforce

We strive to strengthen the ability of Latinos to contribute and share in the nation’s economic opportunities.

Education

We pursue policies and advocacy efforts to ensure equal access to opportunities for Latino students.

Health

We work to decrease health problems in Latinos through policy analysis, advocacy, and partnerships with community organizations.

Imigration

We advocate for fair and nondiscriminatory immigration policies, with the goal of achieving comprehensive immigration reform.

Voter
Registration

We fight against threats to voting accessibility and advocate to make voter registration easier for all.

Wealth
Building

We help Latinos build their futures by making good financial decisions, and pursue policies to increase theiraccess to the American dream.

Youth

Latino youth are the fastest-growing segment of the U.S. population. We help ensure their voices are heard.
